About

I am in the pre-final year of the Data science program at IIT Madras. Currently a research intern (through SPARK) at the CANDLE Research Lab, IIT Roorkee. I was a research intern last year at the CDS department, IISER Kolkata. My advisors are Prof. Sparsh Mittal and Prof. Kripabandhu Ghosh.

I love working on problems with social impact. I'm currently interested in Generative and Responsible AI, NLP, ML.

I'm actively seeking opportunities in research collaborations - studies related to NLP, esp. where there's a scope to explore more on LLMs. If your work aligns with these areas, please reach out, I'd love to join in!
